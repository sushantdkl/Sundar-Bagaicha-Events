About Us

Surkhet's Finest
Dining Experience

Nestled in the heart of Birendranagar, Sundar Bagaicha Events is more than a restaurant — it's where Surkhet comes to celebrate. From intimate family dinners to grand wedding receptions, we craft unforgettable moments with exceptional food, warm hospitality, and a venue that transforms your vision into reality.

Established in 2025, we've quickly become the go-to destination for fine dining, events, and celebrations across the Karnali Province. Our expert team ensures every detail is perfect — from the first course to the final toast.
